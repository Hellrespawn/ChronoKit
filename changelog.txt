Version: 1.0.0
Date: 2026-06-26
  Features:
    - Play/pause toggle button in the top GUI bar.
    - Slower and faster buttons to step through a configurable speed table.
    - Speed display button: shows current speed multiplier, resets to ×1 or restores saved speed on click.
    - Automatic speed reset or game pause when a player-force entity takes damage above ×1.
    - Configurable maximum speed multiplier (2–128×).
    - Configurable damage action: reset speed to ×1 or pause the game.
    - Admin-only controls with in-game message for non-admins.
